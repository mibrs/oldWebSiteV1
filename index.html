My first audio recording

This is a short audio clip done using the Ableton Learning Music website, recorded with Audacity. Its duration was reduced to 5 seconds. Furthermore, I have faded in and faded out at the start respective end. The whole process is also described in a short YouTube video, look out for it.

The idea here was to show you how to incorporate audio clipos into your website, it only requires a few lines of HTML.
